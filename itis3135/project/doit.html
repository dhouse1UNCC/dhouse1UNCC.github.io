THE POT-T COMPOSTING TOILET

Do It

How to construct your own Pot-T!

Required Items

    Tools

  • Lasercutter
  • Socket Wrench

    Materials

  • A large flower pot, approx. 16 inches in height(Recommended)
  • A 5 gallon bucket
  • Standard Oval Toilet Seat
  • Bolts for Toilet Seat

    Instructions

  1. Create a base for the bucket to sit securely on inside of the pot.
    pic placeholder
  2. Create a top that fits over the entire top, with a center hole to match the top edge of the bucket.
    pic placeholder
  3. Place components inside the pot.
    pic placeholder
  4. Bolt toilet seat of choice to the created top.
    pic placeholder
  5. Congradulations! You can now go in style with your own Pot-T!
